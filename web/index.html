opencopper

An open, mine-level world model of copper supply and demand. Commercial models are black boxes behind enterprise paywalls — here, every assumption is a line in a YAML file you can read, dispute, and PR. Built from public filings; shocks are typed events; nothing here is a price forecast.

Tracked: mines individually (~% of world supply), rest-of-world as an explicit aggregate. Two coupled balances: concentrate vs smelter capacity (the TC/RC crisis lives here) and refined supply vs demand (inventory cover → price pressure index). Seed data is estimate-grade by design — the LLM extraction pipeline replaces it with cited values from EX-96 filings. Source, assumptions, tests: github.com/chang-07/opencopper
